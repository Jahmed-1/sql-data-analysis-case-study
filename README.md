# E-commerce Sales Analysis (SQL)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data using SQL to answer common business questions related to
sales performance, profitability, trends over time, and regional insights.

The goal is to demonstrate practical SQL skills used by data analysts in real-world business scenarios.

## Dataset
The dataset contains transactional sales data with the following fields:
- Order Date
- Product Name
- Category
- Region
- Quantity
- Sales
- Profit

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which product categories generate the highest sales and profit?
2. How do sales and profit change over time (monthly trends)?
3. Which regions are the most profitable?
4. Which products contribute the most to total sales and profit?

## SQL Analysis Files
- `01_create_table.sql` – Creates the sales table schema
- `02_category_sales_profit.sql` – Sales and profit by product category
- `03_monthly_sales_trend.sql` – Monthly sales and profit trends
- `04_region_profit.sql` – Sales and profit by region
- `05_top_products.sql` – Top 10 products by sales

## Key Insights
- Certain product categories contribute disproportionately to overall revenue.
- Sales show clear trends over time, highlighting seasonal patterns.
- Regional analysis reveals differences in profitability across markets.
- A small number of products drive a large share of total sales.
